About Us

Nakshi Choya is a textile startup that collaborates with rural artisans, weavers, and potters to create unique, eco-friendly products. Our mission is to empower grassroots artisans, ensure fair pay, and promote eco-consciousness.

Our Products

Hand-Stitched Fabrics

Featuring intricate Kantha embroidery, Gujarati mirror work, and Parsi Gara on sustainable fabrics. Each piece is a unique blend of tradition and modernity, meticulously crafted to perfection.

Premium Apparel

Unique designs on sustainable fabrics, creating one-of-a-kind apparel items. Our premium apparel range is stylish, eco-friendly, and crafted with the utmost care to ensure quality and comfort.
